Contact US

Answers to many of our most frequently asked questions can be found at http://eric.ed.gov/?faq. If you need additional help or wish to make a suggestion, please contact us using the email form below. Please be as specific as possible to help us in our efforts to support you. The ERIC help desk would be happy to provide general search assistance, tips for using the ERIC website, and to answer any questions that users have about ERIC. However, we are unable to provide assistance outside of the scope of ERIC and are unable to provide reference services.
